Rules of Engagement

01

Original Code

All code must be written during the 48-hour window. Pre-built libraries are allowed, but core logic must be fresh.

02

Team Size

Teams must consist of 2 to 4 members. Inter-disciplinary teams are highly encouraged for better results.

03

IP Ownership

Participants retain full ownership of the intellectual property created during the event.

04

Ethical Conduct

Zero tolerance for plagiarism or harassment. Every line of code must adhere to ethical standards.
