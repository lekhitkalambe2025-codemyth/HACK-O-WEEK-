VISUAL_PHILOSOPHY

Prism_View is more than a gallery; it is a study of light as a data stream. By capturing photons in high-contrast environments, we translate physical reality into digital assets. Every image in this collection is optimized for 4K resolution and color-corrected for maximum dynamic range.

SYSTEM_SPECIFICATIONS

Camera: Phase_One XF
Lens: 80mm Schneider-Kreuznach
Processing: Custom Python-based Image-Engine
Encoding: Lossless RAW to WebP.
